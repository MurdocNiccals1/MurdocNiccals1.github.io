VTC History

Founded

Legion Logistics was founded on the 21st of August 2020. The VTC was started with the intention of bringing members from the ETS2 and ATS community together. Our founder created the company not knowing much about VTC's or the work that goes into creating a successful VTC but one thing he knew was that Legion Logistics will on day join along side some of the biggest names in the community. Within a couple days,various drivers from around the world came and registered as a Legion Logistics driver. To this day, players from across the globe are recognising Legion Logistics and are joining the ever expanding community that has been created.

What's Next

What do we plan to do next. With Legion Logistics growing and our driver count growing we will be working to improve our driver satisfaction. We’ll be releasing lots of new features and updates for our drivers, many of which are now being developed. We’re improving the overall quality of our community by adding more of what the drivers want, removing inactive drivers as well as recruiting skilled and experienced staff members. With new additions regularly being added and our drivers beating the roads, the future of Legion Logistics is a bright one.
